Environmental Land Management

Sustainable Farming Incentive

Under the standards programme, you can be paid to complete a set of actions to:

  • improve conditions for wildlife
  • help the environment
  • reduce the costs of agriculture and horticulture
  • reduce the effects of climate change

You will be able to choose standards that are appropriate to the resources you have on your land.
