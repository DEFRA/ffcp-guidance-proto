Guidance

Identify and improve cultural heritage assets on your land

Use this guidance to help you identify and understand the historic features on your land.

Published 26 February 2019
From: Natural England

The guidance deals with designated and undesignated cultural heritage assets. It directs you to where you can find out more about different historic feaures. Your actions can help protect and enhance these historic features. This guidance will help you decide how to best manage historic sites in different land uses.
