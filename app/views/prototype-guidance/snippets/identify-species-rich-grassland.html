Identify species-rich grassland

Use this guidance if you have or wish to restore species-rich grassland on your land.

Published 26 February 2019
From: Natural England

This guidance will help you to decide if your grassland is species-rich. It will also help you to identify which type of species-rich grassland you have. Your actions can help this rare grassland and the species it supports to thrive.

  • Find out how to manage upland hay meadows
